Final scatter plot with clusters. Every dot is a person organized by their cluster group. The further on the X-axis you go the more that person spent money in PCA 1 (Spa, VRDeck, and Foodcourt) and the further on the Y-Axis you go the more that person spent on PCA 2 (RoomService, ShoppingMall).
